How to connect:
  1. Make sure a tunnel mode is active on /setup (Quick, LAN or Cloudflare Named).
  2. Open the GIGI app on your iPhone.
  3. Go to Settings → Pair with Harness and tap the scan button.
  4. Point your camera at this QR. The configuration is automatic.

The /api/pair endpoint is reachable only from localhost to protect the secret.
